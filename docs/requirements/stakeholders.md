# YouTube Research Video Clip Finder - Stakeholders

This document identifies the key stakeholders for the YouTube Research Video Clip Finder project and their primary interests.

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. Researchers and Academics
- **Description**: Professionals conducting research requiring video content analysis
- **Primary Interests**:
  - Efficient search for relevant video content
  - Accurate clip extraction based on research topics
  - Proper metadata tagging for academic citation
  - Time-saving compared to manual video searching and clipping

### 2. Documentary Filmmakers
- **Description**: Creators producing documentary content requiring historical footage
- **Primary Interests**:
  - Finding specific historical moments in longer videos
  - Organizing clips by themes, speakers, or time periods
  - High-quality video downloads for professional use
  - Streamlined workflow for content research

### 3. Content Creators
- **Description**: YouTubers, educators, and other digital content creators
- **Primary Interests**:
  - Quick access to reference material
  - Ability to find specific quotes or statements
  - Organizing content by topics for future videos
  - Fair use compliance

### 4. Journalism Teams
- **Description**: News organizations researching video archives for stories
- **Primary Interests**:
  - Fast retrieval of relevant video segments
  - Accurate transcription and speaker identification
  - Chronological organization of clips
  - Multi-language support

## Secondary Stakeholders

### 5. Educational Institutions
- **Description**: Schools, universities using video content for teaching
- **Primary Interests**:
  - Creating teaching materials from YouTube content
  - Organizing clips by curriculum topics
  - Accessibility features for diverse student needs

### 6. Legal Teams
- **Description**: Legal professionals requiring video evidence or references
- **Primary Interests**:
  - Accurate timestamps and metadata
  - Verifiable sources
  - Chain of custody for video clips

### 7. Project Development Team
- **Description**: Developers, designers, and managers building the system
- **Primary Interests**:
  - Clear requirements
  - Scalable architecture
  - Maintainable codebase
  - Meeting project deadlines

### 8. System Administrators
- **Description**: IT professionals responsible for deployment and maintenance
- **Primary Interests**:
  - System reliability
  - Resource efficiency
  - Security compliance
  - Monitoring capabilities
