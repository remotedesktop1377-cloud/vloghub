# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is the YouTube Research Video Clip Finder?
The YouTube Research Video Clip Finder is a specialized tool designed to help researchers, educators, and content creators find, extract, and organize relevant video clips from YouTube for research and educational purposes.

### Is this tool free to use?
The basic version is free for educational and research purposes. Advanced features may require a subscription. See our pricing page for more details.

### Do I need a YouTube API key?
Yes, you'll need your own YouTube API key to use the search functionality. We don't provide API keys due to YouTube's quota limitations. You can obtain a free API key from the Google Developer Console.

### What are the usage limits?
Usage limits depend on your YouTube API quota. The standard free quota allows approximately 10,000 search units per day, which translates to roughly 100-200 searches depending on complexity.

## Technical Questions

### What video formats are supported for download?
We support MP4 format with various quality options (360p, 720p, 1080p) depending on the source video's available formats.

### Can I download videos with copyright restrictions?
Our tool respects YouTube's terms of service and copyright laws. We provide filtering options to focus on Creative Commons licensed content, but users are responsible for ensuring their usage complies with fair use principles.

### How accurate is the clip detection?
The AI-powered clip detection typically achieves 85-90% accuracy for topic and speaker transitions. Results may vary based on video quality, audio clarity, and language.

### Does the tool work with non-English videos?
Yes, our transcription and clip detection features support multiple languages, with best results for English, Spanish, French, German, and Mandarin. Other languages have basic support.

### How much storage space do I need?
Storage requirements depend on the number and length of clips you download. As a rough estimate, 1 hour of 720p video requires approximately 1GB of storage.

## Troubleshooting

### Why am I getting "Quota exceeded" errors?
This typically means you've reached the daily limit of your YouTube API key. Wait until the quota resets (usually at midnight Pacific Time) or use a different API key.

### Why can't I download certain videos?
Some videos may have restrictions that prevent downloading, such as:
- Age restrictions
- Geographic restrictions
- Copyright protections
- Private or unlisted videos

### The transcription is inaccurate. Can I fix it?
Yes, you can manually edit transcriptions by clicking the "Edit" button in the Transcript Viewer panel.

### My clips are out of sync. How can I fix this?
Try adjusting the sync in the Clip Editor by using the "Adjust Sync" option in the context menu. You can shift the audio forward or backward in milliseconds.
